About Trimly

Trimly is a digital grooming platform that connects customers with professional barbers and grooming experts through a simple, secure, and beautifully designed application. Our web and mobile apps bring together discovery, booking, and payment in one seamless experience, allowing every man to enjoy quality grooming on demand.

We aim to give men the same digital convenience that already exists in other lifestyle services while helping barbers grow sustainable, tech-enabled businesses.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do barbers track their earnings and performance?
Barbers have access to a personalized dashboard that shows real-time earnings, completed appointments, customer reviews, and performance insights. This helps barbers manage their business and grow their clientele more effectively.
Is my data and payment information safe on Trimly?
Yes. Trimly uses industry-standard encryption and secure payment processing to protect your personal and financial information. We never store sensitive payment details, and all transactions are handled through trusted, compliant payment providers.
What if I need to cancel or reschedule my booking?
You can easily cancel or reschedule your appointment directly through the Trimly app. Cancellation and rescheduling policies may vary by barber, so be sure to check the specific barber’s policy before confirming your booking.
How are payments handled?
Payments are processed securely through Trimly at the time of booking or after the service, depending on the barber’s setup. This ensures a smooth, cash-free experience for customers and timely payouts for barbers.
Can I communicate with my barber before the appointment?
Yes. Trimly offers in-app messaging so you can contact your barber before your appointment to ask questions, share preferences, or confirm details.
How does Trimly verify barbers and services?
All barbers on Trimly go through a verification process that includes identity checks, service validation, and profile reviews. Customer ratings and feedback also help maintain high service standards across the platform.
